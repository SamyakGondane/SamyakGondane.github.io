How to Play?

  • Player 1 is X, the other is O.
  • X goes first. Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square.
  • Try to get three of your marks in a row/column/diagonal while simultaneously blocking your opponent from completing theirs.
  • The first to get 3 in a row/column/diagonal wins. If all 9 squares are filled and no one has 3 in a row/column/diagonal, the game is a tie.
